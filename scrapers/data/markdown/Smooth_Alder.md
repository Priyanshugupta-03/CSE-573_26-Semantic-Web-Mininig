## Overview

Smooth alder is a deciduous shrub. The bark has traditionally been used for pharyngitis and intestinal bleeding.

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of smooth alder. Pregnancy and Lactation: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.
