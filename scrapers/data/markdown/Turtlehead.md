## Overview

Turtlehead is a flowering herb that grows in the northeastern United States and Canada. It has traditionally been used as a cathartic and tonic ( 18 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of turtlehead.

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.

#### General

None reported; however, a thorough evaluation of safety outcomes has not been conducted.

There is insufficient reliable information available about the effectiveness of turtlehead.

#### Adult

#### General

The applicable parts of turtle head are the above ground parts and root. Turtle head contains iridoide monoterpenes including catalpol and a bitter-tasting resin ( 18 ).
