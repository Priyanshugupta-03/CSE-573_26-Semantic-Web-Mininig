## Overview

There is very little scientific information about this product. Our staff is continually analyzing the available information on natural medicines and will add data here as it becomes available. KEY HIGHLIGHTS

- Sometimes used for fever, common cold, and cough, but there is no strong evidence to support any use.
- Insufficient reliable information available about the safety and side effects of premorse.
- No known major interactions.

## People Use This For

Orally, premorse is used for fever, common cold, and cough. It is also used to promote sweating.
