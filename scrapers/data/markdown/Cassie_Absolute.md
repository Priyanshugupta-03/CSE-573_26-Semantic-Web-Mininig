## Overview

Cassie absolute is an extract of Acacia farnesiana flowers ( 11 ). It is used as medicine, as a flavoring agent in foods and beverages, and as an insecticide. The plant is grown in tropical areas around the world ( 99898 ).

Likely Safe when used orally in amounts commonly found in foods. Cassie absolute has Generally Recognized As Safe status (GRAS) for use in foods in the US ( 4912 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of cassie absolute when used orally in medicinal amounts or when used topically.

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.

#### General

No adverse effects have been reported. However, a thorough evaluation of safety outcomes has not been conducted.
