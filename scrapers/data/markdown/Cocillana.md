## Overview

Cocillana is a flowering tree native to Cuba, Brazil, and Bolivia. The bark of the tree has traditionally been used as an expectorant, emetic, laxative, and emmenagogue ( 18 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of cocillana.

PREGNANCY: Possibly Unsafe when used orally due to its purported ability to induce menstruation ( 18 ).

LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.
