## Overview

4-Androsterone is a prohormone that is included as an ingredient in a variety of supplements marketed to athletes and bodybuilders ( 91226 ). Prohormones are typically designed to be metabolized to anabolic compounds ( 91094 , 91095 ). Although there is very little known about these compounds, they may cause serious anabolic steroid-like side effects including stroke, kidney failure, and liver injury.

## People Use This For

Orally, 4-androsterone is used to improve athletic performance.

Possibly Unsafe when used orally.  Although the safety of 4-androsterone has not been studied in clinical trials, this prohormone might cause serious anabolic steroid-like side effects including stroke, kidney failure, and liver injury ( 91094 , 91095 ).

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient information available; avoid use.

#### General

There is insufficient information available about adverse effects associated with 4-androsterone. It is purported that 4-androsterone is metabolized to anabolic compounds. Therefore, in theory, there is concern that it might cause serious anabolic steroid-like side effects including stroke, kidney failure, and liver injury.

There is insufficient evidence about the effectiveness of 4-androsterone.

#### CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE

4-Androsterone is purportedly converted to testosterone and other androgens upon ingestion. Theoretically, taking 4-androsterone may increase the risk for cardiovascular events such as stroke and myocardial infarction in patients with cardiovascular disease.

#### LIVER DYSFUNCTION

4-Androsterone is purportedly converted to testosterone and other androgens upon ingestion. Theoretically, taking 4-androsterone may cause liver injury in patients with liver dysfunction.

#### TESTOSTERONE

4-Androsterone is purportedly converted to testosterone and other androgens upon ingestion. Theoretically, levels of testosterone and other androgens may be elevated in patients taking 4-androsterone.

There is insufficient reliable information available about the toxicology of 4-androsterone.

#### Metabolism

4-Androsterone is purported to be enzymatically converted to 4-androstenediol, 4-androstenedione, and, ultimately, testosterone.

4-Androsterone is a prohormone supplement which is purportedly converted to testosterone upon ingestion. Testosterone is a potent anabolic steroid which may be responsible for 4-androsterone's purported positive effects on body composition and muscle strength. However, the effects of 4-androsterone in humans is not documented.
