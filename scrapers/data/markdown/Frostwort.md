# Frostwort
Scientific Name Helianthemum canadense (synonym: Crocanthemum canadense) Family Cistaceae Other Common Names Canada Frostweed, Ciste du Canada, Cistus canadensis, Escarcha, Frost Plant, Frostweed, Hélianthème du Canada, Hélianthème, Helianthemum, Rock-Rose, Sun Rose
|

---

Caution

Do not confuse frostwort with Labdanum (Cistus ladanifer) or Rock Rose (Helianthemum nummularium).

## Overview

Frostwort is a flowering herb that is native to the eastern United States. It has traditionally been used as medicine for its purported astringent and tonic effects ( 18 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of frostwort.

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.
