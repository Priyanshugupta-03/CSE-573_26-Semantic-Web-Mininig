## Overview

Red-spur valerian is a plant that is native to the Mediterranean region. The root of the plant has traditionally been used as a sedative ( 18 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of red-spur valerian.

#### Sedative effects

Red-spur valerian contains valepotriate, which has purported sedative and equilibrating properties ( 18 ).
