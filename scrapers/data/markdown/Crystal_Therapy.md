## Overview

Crystal therapy involves using crystals and gems for therapeutic effects. Characteristics of the crystal or gem such as mineral content, color, and vibration or wavelength determine the type of effect it has ( 16132 , 16133 , 16134 ).

## People Use This For

Crystal therapy is used for improving various mental and physical conditions, and for improving spirituality.

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of crystal therapy; however, there are no known safety concerns.

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.

#### General

No adverse effects have been reported. A thorough safety evaluation has not been conducted; however, there are no known safety concerns.

There is insufficient reliable information available about the effectiveness of crystal therapy.

Practitioners of crystal therapy recommend carrying the desired crystal or gem in the pocket or purse and making contact with it periodically.
