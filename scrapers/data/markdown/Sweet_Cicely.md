## Overview

Sweet cicely is a flowering herb that grows in mountainous regions. It has traditionally been used as a blood purifier, for urinary tract disorders, and for its purported carminative, digestive, and expectorant effects ( 18 ).

There is insufficient reliable information available about the safety of sweet cicely.

PREGNANCY AND LACTATION: Insufficient reliable information available; avoid using.
